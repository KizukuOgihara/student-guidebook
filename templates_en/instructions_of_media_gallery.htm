Instructions of media gallery page

Describes each component of the media gallery.
1.List of playable videos

This is a list of playable videos. Click the video to move to the video playback screen.

2.Search and sort button

Here you can search the video and change the order of the videos.

3.Display format setting

You can change the display format of the video list.(See the figure on the right.)

4.Volume control button

Adjust the volume of the video. A bar appears when you move the cursor over it, so you can fine adjustments. The default is muted.

5.Playback position adjustment bar

Adjust the playback position of the video. Drag to the left to rewind the video, drag to the right to skip the video.

6.Playback speed adjustment button

Change the playback speed of the video. Double speed and triple speed are available.

7.Full screen button

Change the screen size of the video. The button on the left makes the video screen a little bigger, and the button on the right makes the video full screen.
